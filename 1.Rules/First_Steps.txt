====== First Steps ======
Created Friday 13 October 2023
@rules @involvement

	1. **Choose Your Class**: Select one from the four available classes: Fighter, Cleric, Thief, or Sorcerer.
	2. **Understand Your Class**: Learn the strengths and weaknesses of your chosen class.
	3. **Draw the first Adventure Card** (Cavern of Shadows) to learn about encounters and their level ranges.
	4. **Draw the first Encounter Card** (Entrance to the Abyss) for details on what you need to do to confront the main enemy.
	5. **Engage in Battle**: Fight the first enemy.
	6. **Discover Treasures**: After defeating the enemy, discover gems and various treasures.
	7. **Advance and Grow**: As you defeat each enemy, level up and gain new abilities.
	8. **Complete the Adventure**: Repeat steps 3 to 6 for all five encounters in the Adventure.
	9. **Draw a new Town Card:** Transport your party to a new town.
	10. **Interact with merchants** who may offer activities and side quests.
	11. When you are ready, **Draw the next Adventure Card**, **portal back to Oakshade**, or **replay the same Adventure** at a higher level for greater challenges and rewards.
