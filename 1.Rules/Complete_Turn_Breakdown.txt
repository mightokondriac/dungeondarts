==== Complete Turn Breakdown ====

1. **Regroup Stage**
	1. Beginning of "//**Party Turn**//")
	2. Recover Base Darts
	3. Special Abilities that apply to multiple player or are Status Effects that target enemies(as apposed to targeting one of your darts).
	4. apply and/or continue Status Effects 
2. **{player position} Round**
	1. Base Dart Allocation Stage
	2. Attack Stage
		a. Attack Dart Allocation
		b. Attack Dart Evaluation
	3. Damage Stage
		a. Damage Dart Allocation
		b. Damage Dart Evaluation
	4. **Player {pos} Round End Stage**
3. **Party Turn End Stage**
	a. Beginning of "//**Enemy Turn**//"
4. **{minion position} Round**
	1. Minion Action
	2. Minion Total Damage
	3. **Minion {pos} Round End Stage**
5. P**rimary Enemy Round**
	1. Primary Enemy Action
	2. Primary Enemy Total Damage
	3. **Pimary Enemy Round End Stage**
6. **{secondary enemy position} Round**
	1. Secondary Enemy Action
	2. Secondary Enemy Total Damage
	3. **Secondary Enemy {pos} Round End Stage**
7. **Enemy Turn End Stage**
8. **Complete Turn End Stage**
	a. Clean up Status Effects that end at "End of Complete Turn".
